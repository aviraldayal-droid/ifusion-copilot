# High-Impact Demo Questions for TBG AI Copilot

## 📊 Strategic Overview Questions
These questions showcase the AI's ability to provide executive-level insights and data validation:

### 1. **Year-over-Year Performance Comparison**
   - *"Show me the revenue growth trajectory from 2023 to 2025, broken down by mobile voice, data, and mobile money segments"*
   - **Why it's impactful**: Demonstrates real-time data retrieval from multiple time periods and multi-dimensional analysis
   - **Likely output**: Line chart with 3 segments, growth percentages, trend analysis

### 2. **Budget vs Actual Deep Dive**
   - *"How are we tracking against budget for 2025? Where are the biggest variances?"*
   - **Why it's impactful**: Shows variance analysis, business intelligence insights, and executive reporting capability
   - **Likely output**: Variance table, percentage deviations, flags for anomalies

### 3. **ARPU Trend Analysis**
   - *"What's driving the ARPU sortant trends in 2025? Is it pricing, volume, or mix?"*
   - **Why it's impactful**: Demonstrates financial metrics expertise and trend decomposition
   - **Likely output**: Time series, comparison view, insight cards with drivers

3. **Toggle between views** - Show both raw data tables and visual charts
4. **Mention model** - Reference qwen3-coder:480b when discussing analytical sophistication
5. **Emphasize real-time** - Stress that all data is queried live from PostgreSQL, not cached
6. **Show validation** - Highlight how each answer is validated against live data
